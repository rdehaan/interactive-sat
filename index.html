Interactive SAT

In this demonstration, you can try to find a satisfying truth assignment for a CNF formula yourself. The animation shows which literals and clauses are made true and false. Red indicates that a literal or clause is made false, green indicates that they are made true, and blue indicates that neither is the case (yet).

Enter a CNF formula in DIMACS format, in the bottom.
Click "Load" to update the displayed clauses.

Then try to make the CNF formula true by setting the right literals to true.
You can make a literal true by clicking on it.
